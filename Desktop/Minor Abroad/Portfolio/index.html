✦ About me

WHO AM I?

Thought you would never ask!

I'm Xinamayca de Cuba, 21 years old and from the island of Aruba. I'm currently in my 6th semester at Fontys Eindhoven studying ICT & Media Design, where I get to mix creativity with technology in a way that actually means something.

I have a strong interest in user experience design and I love applying design thinking to create solutions that are both functional and meaningful for the people using them.

I chose this minor because it brought together two things I do not compromise on: doing work that has real impact and experiencing the world. Not everyone gets this kind of opportunity and I was not about to let it pass me by. Traveling, connecting with people from different backgrounds and learning about new cultures is something I aspire to do throughout my life.

Outside of my studies I am a pretty outgoing person. You will find me at the gym or deep in a new playlist. I also hope to compete this year in Olympic weightlifting representing Aruba!

✦ Learning goals

WHAT I'M HERE FOR

01
Beyond the Interface

My background has always been on the creative and visual side, so the technical layers underneath have mostly been a mystery to me. This minor is my chance to finally change that — diving into backend development and databases and understanding how the things I design actually come to life.

02
Better Together

My last semester was mostly solo work, so jumping back into a team has been an honest adjustment. I want to actively improve how I collaborate — communicating clearly, pulling my weight, and making group work feel natural again.

03
Show Up, For Real

I know myself well enough to admit that I can take things too easy sometimes. This semester I want to hold myself to a higher standard — finding the balance between enjoying the experience and being someone my team can genuinely count on, starting with the small things.
